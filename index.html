The Tuning Spot
Welcome to The Tuning Spot!

We are Mid-Missouri's first and only full service do-it-yourself auto repair facility. Our bays come fully equipped with access to all the tools, gizmos, and gadgets you will ever need to keep your car running the way it should; all while saving up to 80% on the cost of hiring a mechanic to do the same job!

Not sure how to fix your car or do simple routine maintenance? No worries – all customers can receive FREE mechanic assistance from our experienced team to help you get the job done right! We carry most fluids here on-site and have local parts stores that can deliver whatever else you might need.

At The Tuning Spot we have three main goals:

Saving You Money

With bays starting at just $10/hr there's no end to the savings (many mechanics and big-box repair shops now charge up to $80/hr just for labor). How does spending 8x less sound? Yeah, that's what we thought too!

Comfort and Safety

Lets face it folks we live in Missouri, where its either too hot or too cold. We provide a comfortable solution to your garage or driveway, not to mention the added safety of a professional grade lift.

Education

We aim to provide not only a great place for existing 'gear-heads' to work on their cars, but we also strive to help those people that may not possess the knowledge, tools, or confidence to do even simple maintenance and repairs.

Never worry about getting ripped-off again. Never drive away asking yourself “I wonder if they did the job right”. We are here to help!
