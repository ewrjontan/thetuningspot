About Us
We are a 7000 square foot secure facility featuring eight work bays:
  • Two 2-post lifts
  • One 4-post lift
  • One low-rise scissor lift
  • Four flat bays
More lifts are scheduled for the future!

Each bay rental includes a rolling table and a full set of commonly used tools: SAE and Metric socket set and wrenches, a range of screwdrivers, allen wrenches, pliers and hammers. Specialty tools such as torque wrenches, air-tools and diagnostic equipment are also available for rent. For more info on tools and rental please see the tools section
